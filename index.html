OUR STORY

Serving Authentic Flavors Since 2010

GPS Highway Restaurant has been a culinary landmark, serving authentic Indian cuisine to travelers and locals alike. Our restaurant is a celebration of India's diverse culinary traditions, where every dish tells a story of regional flavors and time-honored recipes.

We source only the finest ingredients from local farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality and authenticity. Each dish is prepared with care to bring you the true taste of India.

Our welcoming dining space provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable dining experience, whether you're stopping by during your travels or enjoying a meal with family and friends.
